Highways England: environment and wellbeing fund

Deadline: 20 October 2020

Who can apply

This fund is aimed at projects looking to:

  • improve biodiversity
  • reduce flood risk
  • improve water quality
  • support the government's ambition to reduce carbon
  • protect and enhance the character of the landscape

The fund is open to organisations from the public, third and private sectors.
